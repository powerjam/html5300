Our Stylists


Kristopher

Stylist

Apropos Salon & Barber has been in the making of the mind of its locally grown owner, Kristopher Powers, for the last 20 years. CUtting hair throughout high school and into adulthood, Krisopher started a family, and began a career in hospitality in 1999. Flash forward to 2013, two years removed from the hotel business, and at a time that was very apropos, Kristopher had the opportunity to open Apropos Salon & Barber within the heralded Sorrento Hotel, and took it. Kristopher brings his attentive ear, sound recommendations, keen eye, and a warm welcome to First Hill.

Loufel

Stylist

Loufel (Loo-FELL), also known as Loofy, has a decade of experience in this industry with men's and women's cuts and color, styling, bridal hair, and teaching the dynamics behind every service. Her inspiration is derived from her advanced training with Aveda, Bumble and Bumble, Davines, Hattori Hanzo, Wella Professionals, and Pravana. She has also created curriculum for cutting color for barbershop education center. She is methodical and pays very close attention to detail in order to provide personalized services for every individual. She's got a ton of passion behind her craft, she will be your personal hair wizard guru.

Darryl

Barber

Graphic designer turned classic barber, Darryl Velasco has a cutting edge and creative, unconventional approach to men's grooming. From customized and tailored classic haircuts and progressive styling to straight razor shaves, he offers an exceptional service for everyone in his chair. The quality over quantity standard is held to the highest regard. Not only is Darryl the guy that stands behind the chair and makes you look good, he is also a consultant, therapist and friend.
